We are Medical Software providers

Nuve SAS

We are healthcare SaaS startup created in Paris, France in 2013. Its purpose is the development of simplified tools for healthcare Practitionners worldwide. The first of them, Nuve Note is a service dedicated to the acquisition, the storage and the reading of medical data (ie Electronic Medical Record also known as Electronic Health Record, EHR or EMR).

Nuve’s claim is « Linking Healthcare Players », as the added value of our services lies in our considering the stakes related to telemedicine, home-aging and, generally speaking, the interfacing of diverse medical devices (Internet of Things). We are considering a sector, which has experienced a limited number of innovations over the latest 15 years (economically speaking, technically speaking, etc.).

EHR in SaaS/h1>

Nuve Note is Nuve’s first product: it’s the first SaaS dedicated to the health practitioners worldwide. Unlike all other software targeting the same customer segment, Nuve Note is dedicated to feature the latest ergonomic and technical breakthroughs so as to allow its cusers to get quickly familiar with it. Another strong disruptive aspect of Nuve Note is his mobile-oriented aspect (smartphones and tablets (iPad, Samsung Galaxy, etc.)). Considering the existing e-health software (SaaS or not), Nuve Note is to be what the iPhone was to mobile phones: a revolution in terms of user experience as well as in terms of business model.

Nuve Note helps doctors and all other healthcare practitionners around the world to manage their clinics, patients and their appointments. Since Nuve Note is available in the cloud, it can be accessed from PCs, Desktops, Mac and on smart phones and tablets using our web apps.
